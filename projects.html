Say goodbye to soggy sandwiches and melted ice! GoSun Chillest is the ultimate outdoor cooler because instead of using ice, it cools food and drinks using the power of the Sun.

The 45 Liter cooler prepares you for any outdoor event with a built-in battery and all-terrain wheels plus thoughtful extras like inside lighting, tie down straps and organization baskets. It also has a built-in compressor that lets you set the temperature right where you want it with a range from -4°F to 68°F (-20°C to 20°C).

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Built-In Battery last?

The integrated 100WH battery can run the Chillest with cold drinks (42°F) on one charge for 10 hours at an outside temperature of 80°F. Generally, when using the Elbow Solar Table in the ProPack, you can maintain charge in the battery.

How much electricity does it use?

The Chillest uses only about 45 watts when the compressor is running. The compressor cycles on and off depending on three main factors: 1) your pre-set temperature 2) the temperature and mass of the contents inside 3) the outside ambient temperature

How does it work?

GoSun Chillest works similar to your household fridge, but much more efficient. A brushless compressor circulates refrigerant through a condenser coil (hot side) and an evaporator coil (cold side) which is embedded in an insulated chamber.

Can I use Chillest in my car?

GoSun Chillest is able to be powered by typical 12 V cigarette ports in the car, boat or RV or with the Extra PowerBank. It can also be run with the included AC adapter. The Chillest has three modes for battery protection to prevent depletion through an integrated LVD (low voltage disconnect).

Can I order multiple items with my pledge?

We work to provide the best pricing and package combinations, but we can't make every imaginable combo. There will be an opportunity to purchase additional units and accessories during the Add-on Survey sent out before delivery.

Can I ship a double order to multiple locations?

Our logistics are good, but not that good. You'll have to receive both units at one address or make two separate orders, perhaps using two logins.

Does International Shipping cover VAT's?

GoSun has backers from all over the world, and shipping fees vary from country to country. We have no control over these charges and cannot predict what they may be. For this reason, the international shipping cost typically does not cover VATs but only covers the cost of delivery to the address provided.

How long does it take to charge the Built-In Battery with the Elbow Solar Table?

This is definitely weather dependent and it will help if you're able to adjust the solar panel to be perpendicular to the Sun every hour or so. Under full sunlight you should be able to recharge a depleted battery in about four or five hours.
